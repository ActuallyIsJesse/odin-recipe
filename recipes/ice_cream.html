Ice Cream

Description

This delicious dish will send your toungue whirling on a ride like it's never experienced before. Get those buds ready, because they're about to be blasted into space!

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Milk
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Put it all in a ice cream maker.
  2. Give it a good whirl for 30 minutes.
  3. Enjoy!
